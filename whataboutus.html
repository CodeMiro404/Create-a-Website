Who are we?

Trainwrecks eSports was founded in 2025 by the founders of the Junantuoma Club association based in Helsinki, Finland. Trainwrecks operates under an independent business name, yet is still part of the Junantuoma Club association.

Trainwrecks eSports shares the values ​​of the umbrella organization and upholds the principles of a safe space in esports as well as outside gaming. The club was founded to provide a platform for potential and competitive youth to gain experience in competitive gaming and at the same time gain visibility for their own talents.

Our mission is to build and maintain a sustainable winning culture and community, create new opportunities for everyone involved, and most importantly, have fun while doing it!
